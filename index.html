Privacy Policy - Calm Journal

Last Updated: 2026-04-16

1. General

This Privacy Policy applies to Calm Journal (Chinese: 解压日记), a pure local offline diary app developed by an individual developer.

We comply with the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China and uphold the core principles: Privacy First, No Collection, No Upload.

All user data is stored only locally on the user’s device. No data is transmitted to the cloud or collected by the developer.

2. Information Collection & Use

We do NOT collect, store, upload, or transmit any personal information, including name, phone, email, device ID, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, etc.

All features run locally without network or login.

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. Any data leakage caused by the user’s own export or sharing shall be borne by the user.

3. Data Storage & Security

All diary content and settings are stored only locally on the user’s device.

The app does not use iCloud or any cloud services, and has no data upload channels.

Users can export diaries in TXT format to save, backup, and share on their own. Uninstalling the app will permanently erase all local data.

4. Permissions & Features

The app does not request unnecessary permissions (network, location, contacts, photos, etc.).

- Security Lock: Local password verification only; no password is uploaded.

- Local Storage: Only for reading and writing diary data and exported TXT files.

- Share: The export function uses the system-native share interface. The entire process is handled by the system; the app does not collect or upload any content.

5. User Rights

Users own and control all local data. They can edit, delete, export, and share diaries at any time.

Users may uninstall the app at any time; all local data will be cleared immediately.

6. Age Limit & Minor Protection

This app is rated 10+.

Minors under 18 are recommended to use the app with guardian consent.

All data is stored locally; we do not disclose any information to guardians or third parties.

7. Contact

Email: hhl330621@163.com

8. Others

This policy takes effect upon publication. Updates will be announced within the app.

All permissions are necessary for core diary services only.
